🔥 Clear Browser Cache

The corridor accessibility fix is working in the API, but your browser is showing cached data from localStorage.

Click the button below to clear the cache and see the fix:

What This Does:

  • Clears localStorage (autosaved floor plans)
  • Clears sessionStorage (temporary data)
  • Reloads the page with fresh data from the server

Manual Method:

You can also clear cache manually by opening the browser console (F12) and running:

localStorage.clear(); sessionStorage.clear(); location.reload();

After Clearing:

  1. Upload your floor plan again
  2. Generate the COSTO layout
  3. Check the red dashed circulation lines - they should be fully connected!
